HOW IT WORKS
OPEN SOURCE
01 📡
WE FETCH LIVE
On every page load, we pull real data from EPA AirNow, NWS weather, USGS stream gauges, WMATA train predictions, and county open data portals. Live cards show a timestamp so you know exactly when data was pulled.
02 📋
WE LABEL HONESTLY
Cards show exactly what they are: LIVE (fetched right now), TODAY (from open data API today), or PUBLISHED with the actual date the government last released that data. No fake daily or weekly badges on annual data.
03 🔗
WE LINK THE SOURCE
Every card links directly to its source portal. Every number is traceable. Built by a Brambleton resident who was tired of digging through 12 government portals to find basic facts about their own county.
DATA SOURCES
FULL TRANSPARENCY
Every number traces back to a public record or live government API. Click any source to open the original portal. If you spot a discrepancy, flag it.
