HOW IT WORKS
OPEN SOURCE
01📡
WE FETCH LIVE
On every page load we pull real data from EPA AirNow, NWS weather, USGS stream gauges, WMATA train predictions, Dominion Energy outages, and county open data portals. Live cards show a timestamp.
02📋
WE LABEL HONESTLY
Cards show exactly what they are: LIVE, TODAY, or PUBLISHED with the actual date. Every card shows its collection method: LIVE API, OPEN DATA API, PDF, Collected, or Static. No fake update badges.
03🔗
WE LINK THE SOURCE
Every card links to its source portal. Every number is traceable. Built by a Brambleton resident who was tired of digging through 12 government portals to find basic facts about their county.
DATA SOURCES
FULL TRANSPARENCY
Every number traces back to a public record or live government API. Click any source to open the original portal. If you spot a discrepancy, flag it.
